Mind You PH

Businesses and institutions have implemented support methods to better assist their employees in order to address the rising statistics surrounding burnout and workplace stress in the Philippines. 80% of Filipino Gen Z and Millennial workers claim that their employers have paid them greater attention since the pandemic began.

I chose this charity to inform and advocate about Mental Health and emphasize its impact on workflow. Spreading awareness about this organization is my objective. Moreover, its approach on mental health is something that interests me.
